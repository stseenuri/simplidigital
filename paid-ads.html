Why paid ads fail

Where most ad budgets
quietly go to waste.

Clicks without conversions

The campaign looks healthy in the dashboard. Impressions are up, CTR is decent. But the phone isn't ringing and the form isn't filling.

Wrong audience targeting

Broad targeting burns through budget on people who would never buy. No negative keywords, no exclusions, no ICP thinking behind the setup.

Landing pages that leak

Ads send traffic to a generic homepage instead of a focused page built to convert. The click was paid for. The conversion never had a chance.

No tracking, no truth

Without proper conversion tracking, you have no idea which campaigns, keywords or creatives are actually driving results. Everything is a guess.

Set and forget campaigns

Campaigns are launched and then checked once a month. Budgets drain into underperforming ad groups nobody is watching or optimising.

Reporting that hides reality

Agencies show you impressions and reach because those numbers always look good. The actual cost per lead or cost per sale stays buried.
