Questions

A few things clients
ask before starting.

How long does a website project take?

+

Most small business sites take 3–5 weeks from kick‑off to launch, depending on content readiness and feedback speed.

Do you also provide hosting and domain?

+

You keep ownership of your domain and hosting. We’ll help you choose and set things up, but everything stays in your name.

Can you redesign an existing site instead of starting fresh?

+

Yes. We audit what you have, keep what’s working and redesign what isn’t, with a clear plan so you know why each change exists.

What about ongoing SEO and marketing?

+

We can continue as your SEO and marketing partner after launch, or hand over cleanly to your in‑house team with documentation.
