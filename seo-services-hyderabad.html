Why SEO fails

Where most SEO retainers
quietly fall apart.

Reports without revenue

You get colourful PDFs showing traffic graphs, but very few people can explain how that traffic converts into calls, enquiries or sales.

Wrong keywords, wrong pages

Your site ranks for generic, top-of-funnel queries while your money pages — services, locations, categories — stay buried.

Technical debt ignored

Slow loading, broken links and messy architecture make it hard for search engines to crawl, understand and trust your website.

Random blogs, no strategy

Content is written “for SEO” but doesn’t answer the questions real buyers ask before they pick up the phone or visit.

Local SEO half-done

Your Google Business Profile, reviews and location pages are an afterthought, so competitors win the “near me” searches.

No clear owner

Developers, marketers and agency partners all touch SEO — which usually means no one is truly accountable for outcomes.

What working together
on SEO actually looks like.

Local SEO — win intent-driven searches around you.

Perfect for studios, clinics, coaching centres, salons, restaurants and service businesses that depend on people nearby finding you first.

What we optimise

  • Google Business Profile setup, categories, services and descriptions.
  • Location pages for each city or neighbourhood you serve.
  • Service-area pages that clearly show where you operate.
  • On-page optimisation for “service + area” and “near me” queries.
  • Review acquisition playbook your team can actually follow.

What you can expect

  • More calls, directions and WhatsApp enquiries directly from search.
  • Visibility when people search with clear buying intent near you.
  • Cleaner tracking of local performance inside your analytics.

On-page SEO — make every key page pull its weight.

Your homepage, services, categories and main blog pieces become focused, search-aligned landing pages instead of generic placeholders.

We refine and fix

  • Titles, metas and headings for each important URL.
  • Page copy, layout and internal linking for clarity and depth.
  • URL structure and navigation so users never feel lost.
  • FAQ and supporting content on pages that need more detail.

Why it matters

  • Search engines quickly understand what each page should rank for.
  • Visitors find answers faster and are more likely to convert.
  • Your site becomes easier to scale as you add new services or locations.

Technical SEO — clean, fast and crawlable by default.

We handle the “under the hood” work so technical issues stop blocking your content and future campaigns.

Core technical work

  • Site speed improvements and Core Web Vitals recommendations.
  • XML sitemaps, robots.txt, canonicals and pagination logic.
  • Fixing broken links, redirect chains and duplicate pages.
  • Indexing audits to ensure the right URLs are visible.

Outcomes unlocked

  • Search engines crawl and index the right content faster.
  • Your site feels smoother on mobile and low connections.
  • New content has a better chance to rank competitively.

Content & strategy — build assets that compound.

Instead of random posts, we create a content system aligned to your buyer journey, from first search to final decision.

What we create

  • Keyword and topic maps around problems, solutions and comparisons.
  • Service pages, city pages, guides and FAQs.
  • Educational blogs that support SEO and sales conversations.
  • Refreshes of existing content that already has some traction.

How this helps

  • You show up when people research, compare and are ready to buy.
  • Evergreen content keeps working long after launch.
  • Your sales and support team can share links instead of long explanations.
