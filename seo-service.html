Why most SEO retainers fail

If you already pay someone for SEO but don’t see clear results, you’re not alone. These are the patterns we see in almost every new audit.

No local SEO foundation

No clear owner

Developers, marketers and founders all touch SEO — which means no one is truly accountable for consistent results.

On‑page SEO: make every important page do its job

We turn your homepage, service pages, product categories and key blogs into strong, search‑friendly landing pages.

What we fix and improve

  • Title tags, meta descriptions and headings for each key page.
